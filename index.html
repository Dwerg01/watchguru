HOW IT WORKS

WE BELIEVE IN CONSIDERED CHOICES

Get your new look delivered, wear it, and then give it back to us in the (v cute) reusable packaging. Doing things the Cult Crush way means you spend less money on buying endless fast fashion - and more time on considered, sustainable ways to look f*kn great.

WE BELIEVE IN STAYING ON TREND

Choose your look from high-end, luxury, on trend pieces, carefully curated from local and international designers.

WE BELIEVE IN BEING INCLUSIVE

Our style may be exclusive, but our sizing is most definitely inclusive. We love you in these looks, babe, from XS to 2XL.

WE BELIEVE IN AFFORDABILITY

Revamping a wardrobe every season is not sustainable or affordable. Renting when needed beats non-stop spending, every time.
